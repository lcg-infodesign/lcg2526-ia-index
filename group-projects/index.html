Group Projects WebPages of LCG (Laboratorio di Computer Grafica) C2 2025-2026, Bachelor Communication Design

Web pages for each group of the course listed by their ID.
Students decided a theme to represent with this quantiative and qualitative visualizations, searching for dataset(s) and defining how to process it.
The main creation are an overview visualization and a detailed one.

  • G1) Affluenza dei Referendum - Referendum Turnout in Italy
  • G2) Stolen Voices (Journalists)
  • G3) Significant Volcanic Explosions
  • G4) World Metro
  • G5) Incidenti Stradali in Italia - Italian Car Accidents
  • G6) Specie a Rischio - Endangered Species
  • G7) The Bubble of Colonialism
  • G8) Nuclear Explosions Archive
  • G9) Food Wastes
  • G10) Liberty Enlightening the World
